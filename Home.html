Welcome to AASHA

All American Sindhi Hindu Association is created to provide a connecting platform for all the Sindhi Hindus that live in All America. To help revive old relationships and build new relationships. To help build and sustain Sindhi Hindu cultural identity for our future generations. We plan to use this platform to organize social events.

Most importantly, help our young Sindhi Hindu generation with a platform to find their suitable life partners from the same Sindhi Hindu Culture families in All America. The young generation group name will be YASH - Young American Sindhi Hindu. We plan to use various social media platforms to get free membership. Increase in numbers will provide the power to create more bonds. So please join this group and share with your family and friends living in All America.
